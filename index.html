Florida Consumer Complaints Per County

The project required to choropleth mapping statistics reports about consumers complains for a variety of companies and services providers per county, and add filtering functionality by company and by product to determine where is the highest number of complaints in which county and which company/product behind that pattern. Using ArcGIS Desktop for geocoding address and spatial join to the Florida counties shapefile, then publish the database as a hosted feature layer to ArcGIS Online and start development the application with ArcGIS web app builderconfiguring filters based on company/product, layer selector, pop-up information and charts.

UN humanitarian summit

Collaborative processes, such as the United Nations World Humanitarian Summit , provide evidence of humankind’s power to visualize change and its resolve to see that change materialize. Our data visualization’s theme, “when spider webs unite they can tie up a lion”, stems from the belief that there are more solutions than problems in the world, when we choose to unite. This data visualization is an exploration of our world. We survey the landscape of both the global bad news (the lion) and the global good news (the spider webs). Then we end our journey exploring the power to unite (human empathy). Through this last section, we find out what people are thinking worldwide and listen to what humanitarians are saying through the World Humanitarian Summit. This visualization represents a sampling of global information to provide an overall picture for discussion and dialogue purposes and it may be viewed from the lens of many different academic disciplines including psychology, sociology, geography, education and more. Our project has ranked between Top 10 Visualizations.
